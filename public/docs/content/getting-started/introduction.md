# Introduction to FaciliScope

Welcome to FaciliScope, a comprehensive Facility Management System designed to help organizations efficiently manage their facilities, assets, maintenance operations, and resources.

## What is FaciliScope?

FaciliScope is a modern, web-based facility management platform that streamlines all aspects of facility operations. Whether you're managing a single building or multiple facilities across different locations, FaciliScope provides the tools you need to:

- **Track and manage assets** - Keep detailed records of all equipment, machinery, and infrastructure
- **Schedule and execute maintenance** - Plan preventive maintenance and handle corrective repairs
- **Manage work orders** - Create, assign, and track maintenance tasks from start to finish
- **Control budgets** - Monitor spending, track costs, and manage facility budgets
- **Ensure compliance** - Track certifications, inspections, and regulatory requirements
- **Optimize operations** - Analyze performance, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions

## Who Uses FaciliScope?

FaciliScope is designed for organizations of all sizes that need to manage facilities and assets:

- **Facility Managers** - Oversee day-to-day operations and coordinate maintenance activities
- **Maintenance Teams** - Execute work orders, perform maintenance tasks, and track time
- **Administrators** - Manage users, configure settings, and oversee organizational operations
- **Finance Teams** - Monitor budgets, track costs, and analyze spending
- **Compliance Officers** - Ensure regulatory compliance and track certifications
- **Executives** - View analytics, reports, and performance metrics

## Key Features

### Comprehensive Asset Management
Track all your assets with detailed information including:
- Asset specifications and documentation
- Location and facility assignments
- Maintenance history and schedules
- QR code scanning for quick access
- Image galleries and attachments

### Work Order Management
Streamline maintenance operations with:
- Multiple user assignments per work order
- Priority and status tracking
- Time tracking and labor cost calculation
- Document attachments and comments
- SLA monitoring and compliance

### Preventive Maintenance
Schedule and automate maintenance tasks:
- Recurring maintenance schedules
- Checklist-based execution
- Automatic work order generation
- Maintenance history tracking

### Budget Management
Control facility costs with:
- Budget creation and tracking
- Transaction recording
- Cost analysis and reporting
- Budget alerts and notifications

### Role-Based Access Control
Secure access with comprehensive RBAC:
- 7 distinct roles (ROOT, ADMIN, MANAGER, SUPERVISOR, TECHNICIAN, ACCOUNTANT, VIEWER)
- Permission-based access control
- Assignment-level filtering
- Multiple user assignments

### Analytics and Reporting
Make informed decisions with:
- Dashboard widgets and KPIs
- Performance analytics
- Cost analysis
- Asset utilization metrics
- Work order performance tracking
