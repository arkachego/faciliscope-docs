# Assets

Complete guide to Assets in FaciliScope.

## Overview

Assets are physical items that need maintenance, such as equipment, machinery, and infrastructure. They are tracked throughout their lifecycle and linked to facilities and work orders.

## What are Assets?

Assets represent tangible items in your facilities that require maintenance, tracking, and management. Examples include:

- HVAC systems
- Electrical equipment
- Plumbing fixtures
- Elevators
- Generators
- Security systems
- Fire safety equipment

## Key Features

- **Lifecycle Tracking**: Track assets from acquisition to disposal
- **Maintenance History**: View all work orders and maintenance performed
- **Location Tracking**: Know where each asset is located
- **Preventive Maintenance**: Schedule regular maintenance
- **Cost Tracking**: Monitor maintenance costs over time

## Getting Started

1. Navigate to **Assets** in the main menu
2. Click **Create Asset** to add a new asset
3. Fill in the required information
4. Assign users who will manage the asset
5. Link the asset to a facility
