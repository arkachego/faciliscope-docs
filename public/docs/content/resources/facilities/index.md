# Facilities

Complete guide to Facilities in FaciliScope.

## Overview

Facilities are buildings, properties, or locations you manage. All other resources are typically associated with facilities, making them the foundation of your facility management system.

## What are Facilities?

Facilities represent physical locations where you manage assets, perform work, and track maintenance. Examples include:

- Office buildings
- Manufacturing plants
- Warehouses
- Retail stores
- Hospitals
- Schools
- Residential complexes

## Key Features

- **Location Management**: Organize work by physical location
- **Asset Organization**: Group assets by facility
- **Budget Tracking**: Track facility-specific budgets
- **Space Management**: Organize spaces within facilities
- **User Assignments**: Assign managers and staff to facilities

## Getting Started

1. Navigate to **Facilities** in the main menu
2. Click **Create Facility** to add a new facility
3. Enter the facility address and details
4. Assign users who will manage the facility
5. Start adding assets and spaces to the facility
