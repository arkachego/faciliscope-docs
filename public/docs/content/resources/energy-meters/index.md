# Energy Meters

Complete guide to Energy Meters in FaciliScope.

## Overview

Energy Meters track energy consumption for facilities and assets. They help monitor usage, identify trends, and optimize energy efficiency.

## What are Energy Meters?

Energy Meters represent devices that measure energy consumption (electricity, gas, water, etc.) for facilities or assets.

## Key Features

- **Consumption Tracking**: Track energy consumption over time
- **Trend Analysis**: Identify consumption trends
- **Cost Calculation**: Calculate energy costs
- **Efficiency Monitoring**: Monitor energy efficiency
- **Reporting**: Generate energy reports

## Getting Started

1. Navigate to **Energy Meters** in the main menu
2. Click **Create Energy Meter** to add a new meter
3. Link to facility or asset
4. Configure meter settings
5. Start recording readings
