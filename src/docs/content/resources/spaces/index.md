# Spaces

Complete guide to Spaces in FaciliScope.

## Overview

Spaces are rooms, areas, or zones within facilities. They help organize assets and work orders at a more granular level than facilities alone.

## What are Spaces?

Spaces represent specific areas within a facility. Examples include:

- Individual rooms (offices, conference rooms, restrooms)
- Floors or levels
- Zones or sections
- Outdoor areas
- Parking lots
- Storage areas

## Key Features

- **Granular Organization**: Organize assets and work by specific areas
- **Space Hierarchy**: Create parent-child relationships between spaces
- **Asset Location**: Track exactly where assets are located
- **Work Order Location**: Specify precise locations for work orders
- **Space Assignments**: Assign users to manage specific spaces

## Getting Started

1. Navigate to **Spaces** in the main menu
2. Click **Create Space** to add a new space
3. Select the parent facility
4. Enter space details (name, type, floor, etc.)
5. Assign users who will manage the space
